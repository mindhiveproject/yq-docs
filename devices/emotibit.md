# EmotiBit

We are working to add EmotiBit support to the YouQuantified platform. In the meantime, you can use it with their software.

## Working With Data On Their Software

You can record and visualize your physiological and motion data using the free and open-source EmotiBit software. Follow [their documentation](https://app.gitbook.com/u/AEQTMLn3qKgOo99JTU5jBSxdKJm2) for a guide on how to get started with using it.
